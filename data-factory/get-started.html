Product-Live Data Factory

Data factory

Product-Live Data Factory allows you to export and import data stored in Product-Live. It basically gives you the power of an ETL (Extract Transform Load) or of an ESB (Entreprise Service Bus) right into Product-Live.
Many of our customers use Product-Live Data Factory to replace tools like:

  • Talend
  • Mule soft
